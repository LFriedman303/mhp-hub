DEQ / SDWIS SOURCING STRATEGY
How we found 224 NC parks that no MHP broker is targeting

Why This Works

Any MHP with 15+ connections on a private well is legally classified as a Community Water System (CWS) under the Safe Drinking Water Act, and must register with the state DEQ and the federal EPA. That registration includes the owner's name, mailing address, and phone — all public record. This list is almost completely unknown to the MHP investor community.
